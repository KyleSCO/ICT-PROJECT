What is Mili?

Mili is a Japanese indie band founded in August 2012, consisting of the main composer, arranger, sub-lyricist, and guitarist Yamato Kasai vocalist and lyricist Cassie Wei/Momocashew, bassist Yukihito Mitomo, drummer Shoto Yoshida, artist and animator Ao Fujimori. Mili covers electronic classical, contemporary classical, and post-classical genres of music and is not limited to songs in Japanese, but also in English, Chinese, and French.
